Solar Power

Energy Conversation is a pretty big deal these days, everyone is trying to opt the E-way of living. At Electra, we added another way of charging your car, through Solar Power. During the day, your car will charge from the solar panels applied to the roof of the car just above the backlights. At night, when there is no sunlight, wireless charging comes to your rescue!
